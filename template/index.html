How we work

ML or AI Projects

Mosaic solves some of the most pressing data challenges facing businesses today. No matter how far you are on the analytics maturity curve, Mosaic can provide additional bandwidth or a quick-win to build momentum.

Rent a Data Scientist

Many firms tell us they have data science projects they’d like to start immediately, but they lack the relevant data science expertise or enough data scientists during peak demand times. If that’s your situation, Mosaic is happy to “rent” you a data scientist (or two) at an hourly rate, with the right skillset, part-time or full-time.

Data Science Capability Development

Mosaic helps companies of all shapes and sizes be more strategic about their use of predictive and prescriptive analytics. We have performed data science assessments for companies in Retail, Supply Chain, Healthcare, Professional Services, Consumer Goods, Financial Services, and Energy.

Business Deployment

Mosaic can provide best practices around data engineering, making sure your technology is aligned with your analytic goals. Our deep expertise developing and deploying decision support capabilities for numerous clients gives us a unique perspective in how to run a successful analytically-driven architecture project.
